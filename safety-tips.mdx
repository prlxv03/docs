## Safety Tips

To stay safe while using Autumn, please follow these best practices:

- Use **alt accounts** instead of your main account.
- Avoid running any **untrusted scripts**, even with 100% vulnerability mitigation in place.

Staying cautious helps keep your accounts and data secure.
