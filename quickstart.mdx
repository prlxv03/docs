## Get Started

Follow these simple steps to set up and run the Autumn scripting utility.

### Step 1: Join the Community

  <Accordion icon="discord" title="Join the Discord Server">
    Join our official Discord to access your key, updates, support, and more:

### Step 2: Redeem Your Key & Download

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Redeem Your License Key">
    In the Discord server, go to any command-enabled channel and use:
    
    ```
    /redeem
    ```

    Follow the prompts to activate your key.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="download" title="Download the Setup">
    Once your key is redeemed, use:
    
    ```
    /download
    ```

    Choose the **Setup** option. This will download the initial application.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="cog" title="Install the Setup">
    - Open the downloaded setup file.
    - Enter your license key when prompted.
    - Once complete, close the setup window.

    <Note type="warning">
      üîê Before installing, **disable Windows Virus & Threat Protection**. The loader uses injection methods that may be falsely flagged by antivirus software.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Launch the Loader

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="bolt" title="Download and Open the Loader">
    - Use `/download` again in the Discord server.
    - This time, select the **Loader** option.
    - Open Roblox and **launch the Loader**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="plug" title="Attach and Enjoy">
    - With Roblox open, run the loader and click **Attach**.
    - Once attached, your script hub is ready. Have fun!
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
